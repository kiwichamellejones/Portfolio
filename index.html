Project 1: DevDiary

Description: DevDiary is a responsive personal blog designed for web developers to share their knowledge, experiences, and insights. It serves as a platform for writing articles on web development, technology trends, and personal projects. The blog aims to provide valuable content to fellow developers and enthusiasts, fostering a community of learning and growth.

Technologies: HTML: For the structure of the blog pages. CSS: For styling and layout, using a framework like Bootstrap or a CSS preprocessor like SASS. JavaScript: For interactivity and dynamic content. Static Site Generator: Jekyll or Gatsby to generate the blog pages and manage content. Markdown: For writing blog posts in a simple and readable format. Netlify CMS: For easy blog post management and content updates. GitHub Pages: For hosting the blog and making it accessible online. SEO Techniques: To ensure the blog is search engine friendly and reaches a wider audience.

Purpose:To share knowledge and experiences in web development. To build a personal brand and showcase expertise in the field. To engage with the developer community through comments and discussions. To improve writing and communication skills.
